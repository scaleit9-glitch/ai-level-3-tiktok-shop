How commissions work: Rates are set by each seller and typically range 10–50%, though they can go from 1% up to 80% depending on the product and brand strategy. You receive payment approximately 15 days after order delivery — not immediately after the sale. Some orders take up to 31 days if the seller has a longer settlement period. Reach 5,000 followers — your growth strategy

Organic Growth Methods

The Account Purchase Option (Know the Risks First)
⚠ Important: Read before deciding
Some creators recommend buying a vetted account with an existing follower base to skip the growth phase. This can save weeks. However, you must understand the risks:

• Buying/selling accounts violates TikTok's Terms of Service — your account can be permanently banned without warning or appeal.
• You have no recourse if the account gets banned after purchase — the money is gone.
• Some "vetted suppliers" sell low-quality or bot-inflated accounts that underperform.

If you do pursue this route: only use suppliers with verifiable track records, verified real followers, and ideally some existing engagement history. Treat the purchase price as money you could lose entirely. This is a risk-reward calculation — not a recommendation.
Fastest safe path: High-volume AI content posting (10–15/day) combined with trending sounds and the teamwork method can reach 5k followers in 3–5 weeks organically for most niches.

7
TikTok Shop Setup for non-supported countries
What to do if TikTok Shop isn't available where you live
+

TikTok Shop is currently natively available in 16 countries: United States, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Ireland, Japan, Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Mexico, and Brazil. Countries expected to launch next include India, Hungary, Peru, and Kenya. If you are outside these regions, you still have options — but they require additional setup. The complete walkthrough with screenshots and current provider recommendations is covered inside the Distribution Tier module of your course. Below is the full overview of everything involved.

Step 1 — Form a US LLC
  • Register a legal US business entity — Wyoming and Delaware are the most popular states (low fees, no local presence required)
  • Services: Doola, Northwest Registered Agent, Stripe Atlas, or a local attorney familiar with international clients
  • Cost: typically $50–$200 one-time state fee + service fee. Total $100–$500 depending on provider.
  • Timeline: 1–5 business days for most Wyoming filings; Delaware can be same-day with expedited processing
Step 2 — Get a US Address
  • Required for LLC registration, TikTok Shop verification, and receiving mail
  • Use a virtual mailbox service: Anytime Mailbox, iPostal1, or Stable — all provide a real street address (not a P.O. box)
  • Cost: $10–$30/month. Use the same address for your LLC and TikTok business profile.
Step 3 — Open a US Bank Account or Payoneer
  • Mercury Bank: accepts non-US residents with a US LLC. Fully online application, no branch visit required. Free account.
  • Wise Business: multi-currency, easy international transfers, connects directly to TikTok Shop payouts
  • Payoneer: TikTok Shop officially supports Payoneer for international creator payouts — simplest route if you want to avoid the LLC entirely for payouts
Step 4 — Register on TikTok Shop as a Business
  • Go to seller.tiktok.com → select United States → register as a business entity
  • Submit your LLC's EIN (Employer Identification Number — obtained free from the IRS after LLC formation)
  • Submit your US address and bank account details
  • Verification takes 1–3 business days once documents are submitted
Important Considerations
  • VPN alone is not sufficient — TikTok detects VPN usage and may ban the account. A legitimate business entity is the compliant and sustainable path.
  • Tax obligations: Non-US residents with a US LLC may have annual US tax filing requirements (Form 5472 + 1120). Use an accountant familiar with international e-commerce — services like Doola Tax or a remote CPA handle this for $300–$500/year.
  • Content device: Create your TikTok creator account on a device with a US SIM or Wi-Fi connection where possible for full feature access
Timeline: Allow 2–3 weeks from start to fully operational TikTok Shop access when going through the LLC route. Factor this into your launch plan.

Reference Photo Strategy
  • Find a "mirror selfie" or casual "girl selfie" on Pinterest that matches your target demographic

Demographic Strategy
  • Age 35–50 for beauty, anti-aging, wellness, supplements — higher disposable income, more transformation-motivated
  • Age 25–35 for fashion, fitness, lifestyle — aspirational content drives clicks
  • Match the model's visible "problem" to the product (e.g., slight under-eye circles for an eye cream, natural aging skin for anti-aging serum)
⚠ Legal & ethical requirement: Disclosure
Creating an AI model designed to look 100% human to sell products is subject to increasingly strict advertising disclosure laws — especially in the EU, UK, and US FTC guidelines. You must disclose that the influencer is AI-generated. TikTok also adds its own "Paid Partnership" label automatically when affiliate links are present. Add a clear disclosure in captions or on-screen text: "AI-generated influencer" or "#AIcontent". This protects you legally and, counterintuitively, often increases trust with audiences who appreciate the transparency. Skipping disclosure risks account removal and potential regulatory fines.

Phase 4 — Build Your AI Content Engine

Master the 3 C's: Capture, Control, Convert. Then systematize it.

11
Write high-converting scripts with AI
The 80/20 method + the 4-part formula that top creators use
+
80/20 RULE Find a viral video in your niche. Take 80% of its structural framework (pacing, hook type, emotional arc, CTA format) and add a 20% original twist (different product, different angle, different hook). You're borrowing proven psychology, not copying content.

Use ChatGPT or Claude to reverse-engineer viral scripts: paste a transcript and ask it to break down the hook mechanism, psychological triggers, pacing, and why the CTA works.

The 4-Part Conversion Formula
H
Hook (0–2s): Stop the scroll immediately"Stop scrolling if you have [problem]" / "I wish someone told me about this sooner" / "POV: you finally found the thing that works"
P
Problem (2–5s): Agitate the pain they already feelMake them feel seen. Name the specific frustration — don't be generic.
S
Solution (5–20s): The product as the rescueShow results, not features. Before/after. Transformation. Keep it short — 7–15 seconds total is often enough.
C
CTA (last 2–3s): Direct and specific"Link in bio" / "Shop below 👇" / "Tap the bag to grab yours"
Example Claude prompt: "Write a 15-second TikTok UGC script for [product] targeting women aged 35–50 with [problem]. Use a strong pattern-interrupt hook. Make it conversational, not salesy. End with a direct CTA pointing to the product link."

Generate 5–10 script variations per product. The hook is the variable — test aggressively.

12
Produce faceless AI videos at scale
The full production workflow from script to published video
+

The standard high-converting TikTok Shop format is 7–15 seconds, no talking head required — a before/after transformation or product demo with voiceover performs best in most niches.

Full Production Workflow
  • Step 1 — Script: Generate with Claude/ChatGPT using the formula above
  • Step 2 — Voiceover: Paste script into ElevenLabs → choose voice matching your brand (energetic, warm, authoritative) → export MP3. Slightly faster than natural speech. Free alternative: TikTok native TTS.
  • Step 3 — Video clips: Use Kling AI to generate product-relevant footage. Or source from brand's listing, stock libraries, or your own product sample.
  • Step 4 — AI model integration: Generate your model with Higgsfield AI or Nano Banana Pro using your reference pose. Drop model clips into CapCut alongside product footage.
  • Step 5 — Editing in CapCut: Sync voiceover + clips → add auto-captions (mandatory — 80% watch on mute) → add trending audio at low volume → add "Shop below 👇" text overlay in final 2 seconds.
  • Step 6 — Export: 9:16 vertical, 1080p, under 50MB for fastest upload.
CapCut template system: After producing your first 3 videos, save your timeline as a reusable template. Subsequent videos become a 15–20 minute swap-and-publish operation.

Build your weekly content production system
Batch creation is how you sustain 10–15 videos/day without burning out
+
  • Monday: Research 5–7 products passing the Profit Formula. Generate 15–20 script variations with AI.
  • Tuesday: Generate all model images and AI video clips. Produce voiceovers in bulk via ElevenLabs.

VOLUME RULE Top creators post 10–15 videos per day at the growth phase. You're feeding the algorithm data about what resonates. Think of it as paying for data with time instead of money. Once a winner emerges, volume can scale back and depth increases.

Phase 5 — Scale, Optimize & Maximize Profit

From first commissions to consistent $2k–$10k/month income.

15
Apply the 95/5 rule when you find a winner
Don't move on when something works — milk it systematically
+
95/5 RULE When a video gets traction (even 10k–12k views is a signal), do NOT move on. Keep 95% of the video identical — the body, the product demo, the CTA, the voiceover. Change only the 5% that is the hook. You're refreshing a proven asset, not rebuilding from scratch.
  • Run 5–10 hook variations on the same winning video body
  • Test: question hooks vs. statement hooks vs. POV hooks vs. "mistake" hooks
  • The winning hook × winning body = your scalable content asset
  • Only retire a video when CTR drops below 1% consistently over 5+ days
Why this works: The algorithm already knows this content's audience. Changing only the hook gives you a fresh bid for new viewers without losing the ranking signal the video has built.
16
Analyze your data — cut losers, double winners
The numbers tell you exactly what to do next
+
  • Watch time % > 50%: Hook is working, content is holding attention — scale this format
  • Click-through rate > 2%: Product-audience fit is strong — apply 95/5 rule immediately
  • Less than 1 sale per 100 clicks: Product landing page or pricing is the problem — switch products
  • High views, zero clicks: Wrong audience or weak CTA — test new hook that speaks to buyers, not viewers
  • Check Affiliate Center weekly: compare GMV (gross merchandise value) per product to identify your actual earners vs. vanity performers
Key metric: Views are vanity. GMV is reality. A video with 5k views and 3 sales beats a video with 100k views and 0 sales every time. Optimize for GMV, not reach.
